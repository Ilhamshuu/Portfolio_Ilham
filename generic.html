Hello there! I'm Ilham, and my interests lie in the fields of data analysis and science. I have completed my undergraduate studies in forestry engineering in July 2023. My journey to study data began intensively after graduating from college by attending bootcamps and training on the internet, especially YouTube and Coursera. As data learning progressed, my interest in this field grew. Digging into data and finding insights that I never thought of before is something that is interesting to me. Moreover, the existence of data which, if processed properly, can even predict the future makes me even more interested.

My journey is driven by the belief that every data point holds a story waiting to be told. I am committed to leveraging this belief to empower decision-making, solve complex puzzles, and uncover opportunities hidden in the vast world of data. For me data is not only there to solve problems and find information in it, but also valuable material that can make the future better.
